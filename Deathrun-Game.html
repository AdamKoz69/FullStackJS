Deathrun Game

Deathrun is a game i am making in Unreal Engine 4 and it has taken me a lot of time to make. It is a game where you need to reach the end of a death trail as fast as possible alive. there are checkpoints, traps, puzzles and secret death routes where it will give you bonus rewards but come at a cost of being more of a challenge!
